Khalid bin Al-Waleed | Undefeatable Warrior


BIO

Full name: Khalid ibn Al-Waleed

Place of Birth: Makkah

Race: Arab

Status: Chief Warrior, General

Alignmet: Good (After reverting to Islam)

Achievements: He won 28 confirmed battles against the Byzantine Roman Empire, Sassanid Persian Empire, and their allies.


Overview of His Achievements

Khalid was a born fighter. He began training at a young age and when he became an adult he ascended to the rank of warrior. His bravery earned him fame all over Arabia and any army that stood in his path was ultimately defeated. Many large empires were afraid of his large courageous army, even the Byzantine Priests warned their people to stay out of his way. But no matter where Khalid conquered he always made peace with the civillians and they quickly came to love him and his religion which many of them accepted, such as Syria, Iraq and the Persian Empire also known as Iran. He truly was Islam's most formidable warrior.
