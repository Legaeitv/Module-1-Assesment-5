Saad bin Abi Waqqas | Knight of Islam


BIO

Full name: Saad Ibn Abi Waqqas

Place of Birth: Makkah

Race: Arab

Status: Commander, Knight, Govenor of Kufa

Alignmet: Good

Achievements: He fought many battles as a knight alongside his leaders and he completed the conquest of Persia.


Overview of His Achievements

Saad was the uncle of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). He grew up in an upper class family where he learned to defend his tribe and homeland. He also mastered the difficult skill of mounted archery. He was officially the Knight of Islam and he fought in six major battles under the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). And he later completed the conquest of Persia after Khalid bin Al-Waleed left to fight the Byzantines. After obliterating the Persian Empire, he founded Kufa, a city in modern day Iraq. Just like his predecessors, he was honest, kind and humble towards the the people and his subjects.
