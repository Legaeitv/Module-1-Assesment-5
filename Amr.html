Amr bin Al-Aas | Conqueror of Egypt


BIO

Full name: Amr ibn Al-Aas

Place of Birth: Makkah

Race: Arab

Status: Governor of Egypt, General

Alignmet:Good (After reverting to Islam)

Achievements: He defeated the Byzantines in all four battles and conquered Egypt.


Overview of His Achievements

Amr became Muslim at the same time as Khalid ibn Al-Waleed. He trained in the army until he reached the rank of General. In 640 CE he took over the rule of Egypt after defeating the Byzantines in all four battles. After that, he built the first Mosque in Africa. Amr was also very just (as Islam orders), and even though he was a Muslim ruler he left the Coptic Christians to continue following their faith without any persecution.
